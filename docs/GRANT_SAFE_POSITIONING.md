# 3C Mall Grant-Safe Positioning

This document is the review lens for README files, Markdown notes, public pages, grant materials, and future copy connected to 3C Mall.

## Core Positioning

3C Mall is a family-centered grocery comparison, meal planning, and household decision-support software platform.

The safest one-line description is:

> 3C Mall helps families compare estimated grocery options, plan meals, and organize household food decisions in one guided place.

## What 3C Mall Is

3C Mall may be described as:

- a software platform,
- a grocery comparison tool,
- a meal planning tool,
- a household food planning dashboard,
- a digital concierge experience,
- a decision-support platform,
- a pilot-tested family support tool.

## What 3C Mall Is Not

Do not describe 3C Mall as:

- a grocery retailer,
- a grocery delivery company,
- a payment processor,
- a medical provider,
- a dietitian replacement,
- a financial advisor,
- an official partner of a retailer unless a written partnership exists,
- a guaranteed savings or guaranteed lowest-price service.

## Safe Language

Use these phrases:

- estimated grocery comparison,
- estimated grocery totals,
- meal planning support,
- household food planning,
- family-centered decision support,
- user-guided planning,
- retailer/provider checkout,
- final prices confirmed by the retailer,
- receipt-based pilot reimbursement,
- privacy-conscious pilot testing,
- general food and wellness information.

## Language to Avoid

Avoid these phrases unless reviewed and approved:

- guaranteed lowest price,
- cheapest,
- guaranteed savings,
- medical advice,
- diagnosis,
- treatment,
- prescribed meal plan,
- we order groceries for you,
- we deliver groceries,
- we process the grocery purchase,
- breach-proof security,
- official Walmart/Kroger/retailer partner,
- HIPAA compliant unless counsel confirms it applies and the controls exist.

## Grocery and Price Claims

3C Mall may show estimates or planning information.

All user-facing copy should make clear that:

- prices may change,
- availability may change,
- delivery or pickup may vary,
- final checkout is confirmed by the retailer or provider,
- 3C Mall does not guarantee final prices.

## Meal Planning and Nutrition Claims

3C Mall may provide general meal planning support. It should not provide medical advice or claim to treat, diagnose, cure, or prescribe.

Use:

> Meal planning support based on user preferences and household planning goals.

Avoid:

> Medical nutrition advice tailored to your condition.

## Family Pilot and Reimbursement Claims

Pilot reimbursement language should stay receipt-based and controlled:

> Families may be reimbursed for approved pilot-related expenses only after itemized receipts are submitted and reviewed.

Do not imply open cash payments, prize money, wages, or reimbursement for unrelated purchases.

## Security Claims

Use careful language:

- controlled storage,
- encrypted storage where available,
- access control,
- backup retention,
- privacy-conscious handling,
- reduced unmanaged-device exposure.

Avoid:

- prevents all breaches,
- breach-proof,
- impossible to hack,
- guaranteed secure.

## Grant Viewer Lens

A grant reviewer should see 3C Mall as a practical family-support software pilot with:

- a real household problem,
- a clear solution,
- a controlled budget,
- a pilot plan,
- privacy and consent awareness,
- receipt-based reimbursement controls,
- safe disclaimers,
- a sustainability path.

## Final Review Rule

Before using any README, Markdown file, public page, grant narrative, pitch deck, or application text, ask:

1. Does this keep 3C Mall in the software/decision-support lane?
2. Does this avoid making us sound like a retailer, doctor, delivery company, or payment handler?
3. Does this avoid guaranteed price, savings, health, or security claims?
4. Does this explain that users confirm final purchases with the retailer or provider?
5. Does this support the grant narrative without overpromising?

If the answer is no, rewrite the language before publishing or submitting.
